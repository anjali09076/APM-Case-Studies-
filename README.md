Case Studies

1. IRCTC Tatkal Booking Redesign

Problem:
8–10 lakh concurrent users during a 15-minute Tatkal window. Payment failures and confusing booking flows led to poor user experience.

Solution:
Smart Payment Retry System + Guided Booking Flow

Highlights:
• User personas and Jobs-to-be-Done framework
• RICE prioritization
• Figma wireframes
• North Star Metric: Tatkal Booking Completion Rate
• GTM and A/B testing strategy

2. YouTube Focus Path

Problem:
Learners often abandon playlists before completion due to lack of progress visibility and motivation.

Solution:
Playlist Progress Bar + Focus Nudge Notification

Highlights:
• Personal pain-point driven idea
• Competitive analysis
• RICE prioritization
• North Star Metric: Playlist Completion Rate
• Premium feature expansion opportunities

3. LinkedIn LaunchPad

Problem:
Freshers struggle with job applications, recruiter outreach, and referral requests.

Solution:
Application Tracker + AI ReachOut Assistant

Highlights:
• Pain-point analysis from real job-search experience
• RICE prioritization
• North Star Metric: Fresher Interview Rate
• Premium feature opportunities
